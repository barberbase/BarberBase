# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Barbershop Owners & Staff**: Require a highly legible, real-time interface to manage the active queue, status changes, and walk-ins.
- **Barbershop Customers**: Need a fast, mobile-optimized view of their current queue status with zero friction and clear WhatsApp notification support.

## Product Purpose
Frictionless queue management and real-time WhatsApp status updates to eliminate waiting area overcrowding, reduce customer drop-offs, and increase shop efficiency.

## Brand Personality
- **Sleek**: Dark, premium surfaces with micro champagne gold highlights.
- **Tactile**: Interactive components that respond instantly to touch (like native physical controls).
- **Chapri-Free**: Minimalist and refined, avoiding aggressive gradients, bright neon colors, or busy AI-generated layouts.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered generic SaaS dashboards with irrelevant widgets.
- Slow, multi-step booking wizards that require registration.
- Bright, overstimulating neon templates and unreadable low-contrast text.

## Design Principles
1. **Tactile Hardware Simulation**: Use physical indicators like machined borders, custom scrollbars, and tactile scale responses on click.
2. **Anti-Halation Aesthetics**: Avoid pure OLED black and blinding pure white. Build depth using custom dark-grey surfaces (Canvas, Matte, Surface, Titanium) and readable warm-toned typography (Alabaster primary text).
3. **Ergonomic Information Density**: Design layout hierarchy that highlights active queue states, wait-times, and status checkmarks clearly without cognitive load.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for body and micro-copy text against elevated surfaces.
- Mobile viewport-fit configurations that prevent unexpected double-tap zoom behavior on physical devices.
- Focus rings and accessible native input patterns for keyboard and screen-reader operations.
